New York, USA

New York is an iconic metropolis known for its skyscrapers, arts, and nightlife.

Top Places to Visit:

  • Times Square: A bustling hub of entertainment and lights.
  • Central Park: A massive green space perfect for relaxation.
  • Statue of Liberty: A symbol of freedom and democracy.
  • Broadway: Home to world-class theater performances.

Best Time to Visit:

Spring and Fall offer pleasant weather; December is great for the holiday season.
