Paris, France

Paris, the capital of France, is known for its rich history, stunning architecture, and vibrant culture.

Top Places to Visit:

  • Eiffel Tower: A world-famous landmark offering breathtaking city views.
  • Louvre Museum: Home to the Mona Lisa and countless historical artifacts.
  • Notre Dame Cathedral: A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture.
  • Champs-Élysées: A lively avenue with high-end shops and cafés.

Best Time to Visit:

Spring (April-June) and Fall (September-November) offer the best weather and fewer crowds.
